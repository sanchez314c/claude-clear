# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

**Contributors to Claude Clear community are expected to:**

1. **Be Respectful and Considerate**
   - Use welcoming and inclusive language
   - Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
   - Gracefully accept constructive criticism
   - Focus on what is best for the community
   - Show empathy towards other community members

2. **Be Collaborative and Professional**
   - Work together to resolve conflicts
   - Listen to community members and their concerns
   - Maintain professional conduct in all interactions
   - Help others learn and grow
   - Share knowledge and experience freely

3. **Be Inclusive and Welcoming**
   - Welcome newcomers and help them get started
   - Encourage diverse perspectives and contributions
   - Create opportunities for underrepresented groups
   - Ensure all voices are heard and valued
   - Adapt communication styles to be inclusive

4. **Be Responsible and Accountable**
   - Take responsibility for your actions and mistakes
   - Learn from feedback and experiences
   - Follow through on commitments
   - Respect community guidelines and policies
   - Report violations of this code of conduct

### Unacceptable Behavior

**The following behaviors are not tolerated in our community:**

1. **Harassment and Discrimination**
   - Use of sexualized language or imagery
   - Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
   - Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
   - Personal or political attacks
   - Discrimination based on protected characteristics

2. **Disruptive Behavior**
   - Spamming or flooding discussions
   - Intentionally derailing conversations
   - Publishing private information without consent
   - Making threats of violence or harm
   - Creating a hostile or intimidating environment

3. **Unethical Conduct**
   - Plagiarism or intellectual property theft
   - Deliberate misinformation or deception
   - Sabotaging others' work or contributions
   - Exploiting community resources for personal gain
   - Violating software licenses or terms of use

4. **Legal Violations**
   - Sharing illegal content or links
   - Encouraging illegal activities
   - Violating privacy or data protection laws
   - Engaging in fraudulent or deceptive practices
   - Circumventing security measures

## Reporting Guidelines

### How to Report

**If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:**

1. **Immediate Response**
   - Remove yourself from the situation if you feel unsafe
   - Document the incident with screenshots or logs
   - Reach out to community moderators or maintainers
   - Do not engage with harassers or trolls

2. **Reporting Channels**
   - **Email**: conduct@claude-clear.org
   - **GitHub Issues**: Report via private issue if needed
   - **Community Moderators**: Contact in community platforms
   - **Project Maintainers**: Direct message to core team members

3. **What to Include**
   - Detailed description of the incident
   - Date, time, and location of occurrence
   - Names or usernames of individuals involved
   - Screenshots or other evidence if available
   - Impact on you or the community
   - Desired resolution or outcome

### Reporting Process

**Our response process:**

1. **Acknowledgment (within 24 hours)**
   - Confirm receipt of your report
   - Thank you for coming forward
   - Explain next steps and timeline
   - Ensure your immediate safety if needed

2. **Investigation (within 3-5 days)**
   - Review all available evidence
   - Interview involved parties privately
   - Consult with community moderators
   - Assess impact on community health

3. **Resolution (within 7-10 days)**
   - Communicate findings and actions taken
   - Implement appropriate consequences
   - Provide support to affected parties
   - Update community policies if needed

4. **Follow-up (within 30 days)**
   - Check on resolution effectiveness
   - Ensure no retaliation occurs
   - Gather feedback for process improvement
   - Document lessons learned

## Enforcement Guidelines

### Consequences

**Potential consequences for code of conduct violations:**

1. **First Offense**
   - Private warning and education
   - Temporary suspension from community (1-7 days)
   - Required apology or corrective action
   - Monitoring of future behavior

2. **Second Offense**
   - Longer suspension (1-4 weeks)
   - Public acknowledgment of violation
   - Mandatory education or training
   - Loss of certain community privileges

3. **Serious Offenses**
   - Immediate permanent ban
   - Removal from all community platforms
   - Reporting to relevant authorities if needed
   - Public announcement of ban

4. **Extreme Cases**
   - Immediate emergency response
   - Contact with law enforcement if necessary
   - Permanent ban from all related communities
   - Legal action if warranted

### Appeal Process

**If you believe enforcement action is unfair:**

1. **Initial Appeal (within 7 days)**
   - Submit written appeal to conduct@claude-clear.org
   - Provide additional context or evidence
   - Explain why action was unjustified
   - Request review by different moderator

2. **Review Process (within 5 days)**
   - Different moderators review the case
   - Consider all new information
   - Consult with community advisory board
   - Make final determination

3. **Final Decision**
   - Communicate outcome and reasoning
   - Uphold, modify, or reverse original action
   - Document decision for future reference
   - No further appeals available

## Community Guidelines

### Communication Standards

**In all community interactions:**

1. **Written Communication**
   - Use clear, concise language
   - Avoid sarcasm or ambiguous humor
   - Consider cultural and language differences
   - Use appropriate formatting for readability
   - Include content warnings when needed

2. **Video and Voice Communication**
   - Ensure appropriate background and attire
   - Use respectful language and tone
   - Allow others to speak without interruption
   - Follow meeting etiquette and guidelines
   - Record sessions with consent when needed

3. **Social Media and Public Platforms**
   - Represent community positively
   - Maintain professional conduct
   - Respect privacy of community members
   - Follow platform-specific guidelines
   - Clarify personal vs. community opinions

### Contribution Guidelines

**When contributing to Claude Clear:**

1. **Code Contributions**
   - Follow project coding standards
   - Write clear, documented code
   - Include appropriate tests
   - Respect existing architecture
   - Welcome feedback and suggestions

2. **Documentation Contributions**
   - Ensure accuracy and completeness
   - Use inclusive and accessible language
   - Follow documentation style guides
   - Include examples and tutorials
   - Update related documentation

3. **Community Support**
   - Provide helpful, accurate information
   - Admit when you don't know something
   - Direct people to appropriate resources
   - Be patient with newcomers
   - Share knowledge and experiences

## Conflict Resolution

### Constructive Disagreement

**When disagreements arise:**

1. **Assume Good Intent**
   - Give others benefit of doubt
   - Ask for clarification before reacting
   - Consider different perspectives
   - Focus on issues, not personalities
   - Seek common ground

2. **Productive Discussion**
   - Use "I" statements for personal views
   - Provide evidence and reasoning
   - Listen actively and respectfully
   - Be open to changing your mind
   - Agree to disagree when necessary

3. **Escalation Process**
   - Try direct resolution first
   - Involve neutral third party if needed
   - Request moderator mediation
   - Use formal reporting for serious issues
   - Accept and implement resolutions
